About Maneki-Neko Memes

The Maneki-Neko (招き猫), or "beckoning cat," is a Japanese figurine believed to bring good luck. We've taken this iconic symbol and turned it into the ultimate meme star.

Our collection blends traditional Japanese culture with modern internet humor—because nothing says "good fortune" like a well-timed meme.
